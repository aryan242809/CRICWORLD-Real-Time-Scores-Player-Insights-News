Virat Kohli


Description :
Virat Kohli is a world-renowned Indian cricketer known for his aggressive batting style, consistency, and passion for the game. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, he rose to fame after leading India to victory in the 2008 U-19 World Cup. He has scored over 25,000 international runs and holds the record for the most centuries in ODI cricket. A former captain of the Indian team, Kohli is admired for his fitness, sharp fielding, and ability to chase targets under pressure. Off the field, he's known for his discipline, brand endorsements, and marriage to actress Anushka Sharma.
