Rohit Sharma


Description :
Rohit Sharma is one of India’s most successful and elegant batsmen, known for his effortless stroke play and record-breaking performances. Born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, he made his international debut in 2007. He holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs—264 runs. As captain, he has led Mumbai Indians to multiple IPL titles and now leads the Indian national team in all formats. Rohit is widely respected for his calm demeanor, sharp cricketing brain, and consistent performances, particularly in white-ball cricket.
